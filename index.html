Itinerary

Five days through the wine route, from Strasbourg to the southern villages. Pace is relaxed: long lunches, sunset views, and at least one dégustation per day.

Day 1 · Friday

17 July · Drive south & arrive in Colmar

  • 07:00 Depart from the Netherlands. Coffee, snacks, road-trip playlist on.
  • 10:30 Pit stop near Luxembourg / Metz — lunch break.
  • 15:00 Arrive in Colmar, our base. Check in, drop bags.
  • 16:30 Stroll La Petite Venise, get oriented, espresso on a square.
  • 19:30 Welcome dinner — Alsatian classic tarte flambée & a bottle of Riesling.

🚗 ~7 hrs driving (≈ 650 km). Split the wheel between Juup and one co-pilot.

Day 2 · Saturday

18 July · The Wine Route — northern villages

  • 09:30 Drive to Riquewihr — fairytale village, walk the ramparts.
  • 11:00 First tasting at a Grand Cru domaine (Schoenenbourg).
  • 13:00 Lunch in Hunawihr — local baeckeoffe or choucroute.
  • 15:00 Visit Ribeauvillé; quick stop at a Pinot Noir cellar.
  • 17:30 Detour to Château du Haut-Kœnigsbourg for sunset views over the plain.
  • 20:30 Dinner back in Colmar — winstub atmosphere.

🍷 Aim for max 2 tastings; spit, hydrate, designate a driver.

Day 3 · Sunday

19 July · Strasbourg — culture & cathedral

  • 09:00 Drive up to Strasbourg (~45 min).
  • 10:30 Climb Cathédrale Notre-Dame; catch the astronomical clock at 12:30.
  • 13:00 Long lunch in La Petite France.
  • 15:00 Pick one: Musée d'Art Moderne or Palais Rohan.
  • 17:00 Boat tour on the Ill (Batorama) — easy way to see the European Quarter.
  • 20:00 Apéro on Place Kléber, dinner in the old town.

🏛️ Sunday: confirm museum opening hours in advance.

Day 4 · Monday

20 July · Work morning, play afternoon

  • 08:30 Coffee & co-working from the gîte / a Colmar café with wifi.
  • 09:00 – 13:00 💻 Focused work block — laptops out, async standup, ship some code.
  • 13:30 Quick lunch — flammekueche to-go.
  • 14:30 Drive south to Eguisheim (most beautiful village in France).
  • 16:00 Tasting at a small biodynamic domaine (Gewürztraminer focus).
  • 18:00 Walk the concentric streets, find the storks' nests.
  • 20:30 Dinner with a view of the vineyards.

🛜 Pre-check wifi at the accommodation. Backup: 4G hotspot from phones.

Day 5 · Tuesday

21 July · Last sips & drive home

  • 08:00 Breakfast, pack the car, load the wine boxes carefully. 📦
  • 09:30 One final stop in Kaysersberg for a coffee and a last walk.
  • 11:00 Hit the road north.
  • 14:00 Lunch break around Metz / Luxembourg.
  • 19:00 Home in the Netherlands. 🏠

🧊 Bring a cooler bag — summer heat is not friendly to white wine in the trunk.

Logistics

🚗 Travel

By car from the Netherlands. ~650 km / ~7 hrs each way via Luxembourg & Metz. Tolls in France — keep some euros & a card handy.

🏡 Base

Colmar — central on the Wine Route, easy day trips to Strasbourg, Eguisheim, Riquewihr.

💻 Work setup

Monday morning is a work block. Pick a place with reliable wifi; bring chargers, headphones, and a hotspot as backup.

🍷 Wine plan

Max two tastings per day. Spit at the first, sip at the second. Box per person to bring home — ~12 bottles fits comfortably.

🎟️ Book ahead

Cathedral platform tickets, Batorama boat, and at least one domaine tasting (esp. Saturday).

🌡️ Weather

July in Alsace: warm days (25–30°C), cool evenings. Light layers, comfy shoes, sunscreen.
